# Privacy Policy for Tobedo

**Last updated:** 2026-03-19

Tobedo (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a personal productivity app that helps you manage tasks and focus sessions. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
Tobedo **does not require an account** to use the core app experience, and we **do not** collect account details like email/password for normal app usage.

If we introduce sign-in in the future, we will update this policy.

### 2. Task & Focus Session Data (Stored on Your Device)
Tobedo stores the following **locally on your device**:

- Task titles
- Task completion status
- Associated task dates
- Task ordering (if you reorder tasks)
- Focus session (Pomodoro) state needed for continuity

This data is stored using device storage (for example, **AsyncStorage / SecureStore** in React Native). We do not transmit your tasks or focus data to our servers.

### 3. Onboarding State
On first launch, Tobedo stores a small local flag to remember that you’ve completed onboarding (so the onboarding screen shows only once).

### 4. Usage Data / Analytics
We do **not** currently use analytics SDKs or tracking for advertising or behavior profiling.

## How We Use Your Information
We use locally stored data only to:

- Save and display your tasks and focus sessions
- Preserve app state (e.g., completed onboarding, focus session continuity)
- Keep the app functioning reliably

We do not sell your data.

## Data Sharing
We do **not** share your task data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Retention

- Task and focus-session data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear app data, or uninstall Tobedo.
- The onboarding-completed flag remains on your device unless you clear app data or reinstall.

## Your Choices

- You can add/delete tasks at any time within the app.
- You can remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from your device settings.

## Children’s Privacy
Tobedo is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
