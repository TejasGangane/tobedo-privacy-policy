# Privacy Policy for Tobedo

Last updated: 2025-03-13

Tobedo (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a personal productivity app that helps you manage tasks and focus sessions. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

We use Clerk as our authentication provider. When you sign up or sign in, Clerk may collect:

- Email address  
- Authentication information (such as passwords or one-time codes)  
- Technical information related to login security  

We do not store your password ourselves. Authentication is handled by Clerk. For more details, see Clerk’s own privacy policy.

### 2. Task Data

We store:

- Task titles  
- Task completion status  
- Associated dates  

This task data is stored **locally on your device** using secure storage technologies provided by the operating system (for example, AsyncStorage / SecureStore in React Native). We do not transmit your tasks to our own servers.

### 3. Usage Data

We do not currently use any analytics SDKs or tracking for behavior profiling or advertising.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

- Authenticate you and allow you to access your account  
- Store and display your tasks and focus sessions  
- Keep the app functioning and secure  

We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Data Sharing

We may share limited data with:

- **Clerk**: for authentication and security.

We do not share your tasks with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Retention

- Task data is kept on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.  
- Authentication data is managed by Clerk, according to their own retention policies.

## Your Choices

- You can delete tasks at any time within the app.  
- You can sign out or delete your account via Clerk’s account management (if exposed in the app) or by contacting us.  

## Children’s Privacy

Tobedo is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
